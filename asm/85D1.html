85D1: Airborne status indicator
Initialised by the routine at 87CA, checked by the routines at 8B07 and 95C8, updated by the routines at 90B6, 91BE and 94B0, and checked and updated by the routines at 8DD3, 8ED4 and 94D2. Possible values are:
Value Meaning
0 Willy is neither falling nor jumping
1 Willy is jumping
2-11 Willy is falling, and can land safely
12-15 Willy is falling, and has fallen too far to land safely
255 Willy has collided with a nasty, an arrow, a guardian, or Maria (see 90B6)
