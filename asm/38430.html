38430: Check and set the attribute byte for a cell occupied by Willy's sprite
Used by the routine at 38344. Sets the attribute byte in the buffer at 23552 for one of the six cells (in three rows of two) occupied by or under Willy's sprite, or kills Willy if the cell contains a nasty. On entry, C holds either 15 if the cell is in the top two rows, or Willy's pixel y-coordinate if the cell is in the bottom row.

C 15 or Willy's pixel y-coordinate
HL Address of the attribute byte in the buffer at 23552
